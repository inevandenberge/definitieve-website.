About

Ine Van Den Berge was born in Belgium (1996) and studies fine arts at Luca school of Arts in Ghent - more specifically painting.

During her second year of this program however, she discovered that painting did not give her any artistic fulfillment. She learnt that she was gravitating more towards 3D work.

Using cotton, foam and HAMA-beads she creates everyday objects that are then painted using acrylic paint. All these objects represent the past and nostalgia. They are representations of her childhood memories.
